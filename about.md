# About Hamish

Hi, I'm a Data Scientist based in London, UK.

If you want one sentence describing me it's "always wanting to learn". For me, a day where you aren't learning is a day wasted. I think for that to be maintainable you should pursue what you're interested in, if you are tired of a topic it's fine to put it down for a while and you shouldn't feel guilty. I think my work and my career and my interests reflect that.

I spend a lot of time on [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/hamishdickson), which while I would like more medals I really use for learning purposes. One day I'll write a blog post expanding on this.

My background is in physics, I was drawn to physics because it's the purest form of "how does *that* work" I know of. I've always been good at maths, but physics was what captured my imagination when I was younger.

After doing a Master's at Imperial College London I decided not to pursue a PhD (a decision I still question 15 years later) and instead try to earn a bit of money. Computer things seemed like a logical choice and after teaching myself enough code to pass an interview I fell into a financial software job. 3 or 4 job moves later I'm not a data scientist at OVO Energy.

I spend a lot of my time thinking about NLP and sequences of data.
